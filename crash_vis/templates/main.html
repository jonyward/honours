Welcome to my Honours Project!



This application takes data collected by the UK goverment, available here, and visualises it through a leaflet.js map
making the location of all accidents recorded in 2021 on UK roads, as well as the density of accidents in specific.

The goal of this website is to effectively visualise this data to better inform people on roads
and locations which could be potentially dangerous to drive on based on previous accidents, while also being easy to use and accessible through a website.

Two interactable maps are available, one which shows indiviual locations of accidents, and interactable clusters of markers,
with another map available showing a HeatMap of all crashes, giving a better idea of conveying the density of accidents in
specific areas.
